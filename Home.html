About Us

Who We Are

We are a dynamic Bible-based ministry aimed at growing dominant and impactful generations in Christ, through shaping culture and influencing societies.

Our Mission

  • EQUIP THE NEXT GENERATION WITH LEADERSHIP, FAITH AND LIFE SKILL
  • STRENGTHEN MARRIAGES AND FAMILIES THROUGH KINGDOM PRINCIPLES
  • EMPOWER STUDENTS TO LIVE BOLD, UNCOMPROMISING KINGDOM LIVES IN THEIR SCHOOLS/UNIVERSITIES
  • ESTABLISH A CHURCH CULTURE THAT CELEBRATES EXCELLENCE, INTEGRITY, INNOVATION, GROWTH, LOVE, HONOUR AND FAITH.

Our Vision

TO RAISE A GENERATION OF IMPACTFUL, SPIRIT-EMPOWERED YOUNG LEADERS WHO WILL SHAPE CULTURE, INFLUENCE SOCIETY, AND ADVANCE GOD'S KINGDOM - STARTING IN PRETORIA CENTRAL AND REACHING THE NATION.
